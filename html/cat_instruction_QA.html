Quality assurance

CAT12 use different approaches to allow quality control.

  • Preprocessing results and image quality marks

    In preprocessing each dataset is evaluated for image quality resulting in one single school mark that describes the expected image quality (1 - very good, 2 - good, 3 - acceptable, 4 - marginal, >5 - problematic). The results were printed on the command window and within the SPM graphic figure, with other parameters, preprocessing results and images of the original T1 image, the corrected T1 image, and the final segmentation. This figure is saved within the subject directory under 'cat_*.ps', whereas the values were stored as xml-file 'vbm_*.xml' that can be opened by vbm_io_xml. You can find further information in the QA methods section.
