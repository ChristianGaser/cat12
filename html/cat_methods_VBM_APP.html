Affine PreProcessing:

APP was developed to process animal data with the standard CAT pipeline. It include an simple initial inhomogeneity correction to improve the first initial affine registration to start with a skull-stripping that is required as mask in the initial SPM preprocessing. The skull-stripping also includes a second finer inhomogeneity correction that is further used in the preprocessing. There are four options:

  • APP0: no APP and only standard preprocessing (default)
  • APP1:
  • APP2: full without affine scaling (that caused problems in smaller primates)
  • APP3: full
