White Matter Hyperintensity Correction: WMHC=0

Without correction, WHMs were often segmented as GM that causes problems in the non-linear registration and surface reconstruction. The non-linear registration try to moves the WMHs to the closest (cortical) GM regions, whereas the surface reconstruction handles the WMHs like (blurred) sulci, resulting in large topological (and geometrical) defects.
