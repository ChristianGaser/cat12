White Matter Hyperintensity Correction: WMHC=2

With WMHC=2 correction, WHMs were permanently defined as WM, which solves the registration and surface reconstruction problems and gives a more correct tissue classification although the WM tissue degeneration is not taken into account (more similar to SPM8 that is stronger biased by the probability maps).
