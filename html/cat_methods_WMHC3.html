White Matter Hyperintensity Correction: WMHC=3

With WMHC=3, WHMs are assigned to a new class (that can be also exported in expert mode), which solves the registration and surface reconstruction problems, resulting in a more correct tissue classification that takes the WM tissue degeneration into account.
