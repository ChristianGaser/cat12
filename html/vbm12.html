Description

This toolbox is a an extensions to SPM12 (Wellcome Department of Cognitive Neurology) to provide voxel-based morphometry (VBM). It is developed by Christian Gaser and Robert Dahnke (Jena University Hospital, Departments of Psychiatry and Neurology) and free but copyright software, distributed under the terms of the GNU General Public Licence.
