Templates

VBM12 contains different templates to process special human groups and other species. The templates can be changed by running the vbm_set_cg_vbm_defaults routine before the preprocessing.

The template creation process is still under development and the templates were on an early stage (alpha). The contain a TPM map for the tissue segmentation, T1/PD and a brain mask for registration, a species specific version of the VBM12 atlas map.

The following templates are available:
  • primate400 for chimpanzees, bonobos, orang-utan, and gorillas
  • primate130 for baboons and macaque monkeys
  • primate45 for gibbons
  • primate15 for squirrels

The primates templates based on the Rilling dataset (Rilling19xx), and due to the low number of subjects we mixed some species with similar brain size for the templates. This is not so critical because VBM use an AMAP segmentation for final tissue classification and was finally the best way to start with this topic. Therefore, we recommend to create further Dartel templates based on the affine VBM output. The TPM maps can also be used for SPM preprocessing.
