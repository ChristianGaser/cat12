VBM12 > Methods > Surface-based preprocessing

Cortical surface and thickness estimation (in development):

VBM12 also include the estimation of the cortical thickness and central surface of the left and right hemispheres based on the PBT method (Dahnke et al. 2012). Furthermore, the surface pipeline uses topology correction (Yotter et al. 2011a), spherical mapping (Yotter et al. 2011b) and estimation of local surface complexity (Yotter et al. 2011c) and local gyrification (Luders et al. 2006).

  2. Topology Correction

    Although, the initial PBT surface looks still quite good, it often contains small defects in problematic regions like the hypocampus that have to be removed for a correct spherical projection to the average surface. VBM12 uses a topology correction that based on spherical harmonics to replace affected regions by low bandwidth representations of these regions.
