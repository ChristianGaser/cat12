Links, sources, and recommendations

  • IXI database
    Nice Dataset of anatomical image different modalities of over 500 healthy subjects with user friendly terms of use.

  • BWP (Brain Web Phantom)
    Most important validation dataset of structural methods (Collins 1998).

  • Hammers atlas
    Nice anatomical atlas of 20 healthy subjects that is used within this toolbox.

  • Anatomy toolbox
    SPM toolbox with cytoarchitectonic atlas maps.

  • MS (Multiple Sclerosis) toolbox
    Estimation of MS lesions in T1+T2 images.

  • BrainAge
